Lemongrass Chili Tofu

Ingredients

  • 600 g / 1.3 lb fried tofu
  • 4 stalks lemongrass
  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 6 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 chili

Steps

  1. Cut up the fried tofu into smaller chunks (or leave as is if preferred).
  2. Marinate the tofu with the lemongrass, honey, salt, soy sauce and chili for a minimum of 5 minutes.
  3. Oil a wok on medium to high heat, then sear the tofu for 3 minutes on both sides or until golden brown.
  4. Serve the Lemongrass Chili Tofu as is over rice!
